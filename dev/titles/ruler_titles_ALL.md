# ARMONORICAN (Celtique — Gallois/Breton/Cornique)
# ============================================================
# 2 dialectes: Aggeuios (rural archaique) + Ebiam (urbain poli)
# Design: Celtic political vocabulary from different Celtic branches
# Aggeuios = archaic with exclusive markers (Rh-, dd, -ydd, gh)
# Ebiam = smoothed urban forms (Ll->L, Rh->R, dd->d, gh dropped)

## Hierarchy: Pendraig (dragon) > Brenin (king) > Tiern (lord) > Distain (steward)

| Rank    | Aggeuios M     | Aggeuios F      | Ebiam M    | Ebiam F     | Origin                      |
|---------|----------------|-----------------|------------|-------------|------------------------------|
| Empire  | Pennddraig     | Pennddraiges    | Pendraig   | Pendraiges  | pen+draig = chef-dragon      |
| Kingdom | Brenhydd       | Brenhyddes      | Brenin     | Brenhines   | gallois brenin = roi         |
| Duchy   | Tighern        | Tighernes       | Tiern      | Tiernes     | breton *tigernos = seigneur  |
| County  | Rhyddystain    | Rhyddystaines   | Distain    | Distaines   | gallois distain = intendant  |

## What Ebiam smooths:
# nn->n, dd->d (Pennddraig -> Pendraig)
# -ydd->-in (Brenhydd -> Brenin)
# gh dropped (Tighern -> Tiern)
# Rh- and dd- dropped, y->i (Rhyddystain -> Distain)
